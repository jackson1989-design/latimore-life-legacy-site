What This Solves

Many families in Schuylkill County face a retirement gap — not because they aren’t saving, but because traditional retirement accounts are exposed to market volatility and future tax increases. Tax-advantaged wealth accumulation solves this by creating a protected, tax-efficient reservoir of cash that grows steadily and can be accessed tax-free.

Why It Matters in Schuylkill County

With a median household income of $66,901 and a workforce heavily concentrated in manufacturing and logistics, residents often lack access to employer-sponsored financial planning. Market downturns hit hard, and many families cannot afford to lose retirement savings. A tax-advantaged strategy provides stability, predictability, and long-term security.

How the Strategy Works

We use a modern, engineered form of life insurance — typically an Indexed Universal Life (IUL) policy — designed for maximum cash accumulation. Your contributions grow tax-deferred, and you can access the cash value tax-free through participating policy loans. Meanwhile, your principal is protected from market loss.

Our preferred solution is the North American Builder Plus IUL® 4, which includes a Fixed Interest Participating Loan feature that allows your money to continue earning interest even while borrowed against.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the same as a 401(k) or IRA?

No. This strategy is not tied to the stock market and offers tax-free access instead of tax-deferred withdrawals.

Can I lose money?

Your principal is protected from market downturns. You cannot lose money due to market performance.

Is this only for high-income earners?

No. Many middle-income families use this strategy to build long-term financial security.
