Founder Story

Latimore Life & Legacy was founded by Jackson Montgomery Latimore following a life-altering cardiac event that reshaped his understanding of preparation, protection, and continuity.

This experience became the foundation of the firm’s philosophy: life continues, families continue, and the work of protecting them must continue as well.

The Beat Goes On

This internal guiding principle represents resilience, responsibility, and long-term stewardship. It informs the firm’s tone, mission, and long-term vision.
