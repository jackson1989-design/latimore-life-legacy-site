My Story: Why Protection Matters

December 7, 2010 – East Stroudsburg University

During a college basketball game, my heart stopped. I went into sudden cardiac arrest on the court at ESU’s Koehler Fieldhouse. I was 22 years old.

I survived because two athletic trainers and an EMT had immediate access to an AED and knew exactly what to do.

That AED was there because of the Gregory W. Moyer Defibrillator Fund — preparation saved my life.

That experience taught me this: protection isn’t about selling policies — it’s about being ready when the unexpected happens.

Today, I help families prepare financially with the same mindset that saved me physically.
