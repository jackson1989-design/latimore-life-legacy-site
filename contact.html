Common Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Not necessarily! Feel free to call or text anytime. If you want guaranteed focused time, booking a consultation ensures we have dedicated time for your questions.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes! Completely free, no obligation. We'll discuss your situation, answer questions, and provide education. You're under zero pressure to buy anything.

Can we meet virtually?

Absolutely! Phone, video call, or in-person - whatever works best for you. Most initial consultations happen by phone or video.

What areas do you serve?

Primary service area: Schuylkill, Luzerne, and Northumberland Counties in Pennsylvania. Licensed throughout PA and can work with clients statewide via virtual meetings.

How do you get paid?

I'm compensated by insurance carriers when policies are issued - there's never a fee charged to clients. As an independent advisor, I'm free to recommend the best solution regardless of commission structure.
