Our Products

Protection-first insurance and investment solutions for Pennsylvania families

Life Insurance

Coverage designed to replace income, pay off debts, and protect your family's financial future

Term Life Insurance

Affordable, straightforward protection for a specific period (10, 20, or 30 years). Perfect for covering mortgages, income replacement during working years, or protecting young families.

  • Lowest cost per dollar of coverage
  • Fixed premiums for the entire term
  • Options to convert to permanent coverage
  • Living benefits riders available
  • Coverage amounts from $100K to $1M+
Get Instant Quote

Whole Life Insurance

Permanent coverage that lasts your entire life with guaranteed cash value growth. Provides both death benefit protection and a savings component.

  • Lifetime protection that never expires
  • Guaranteed cash value accumulation
  • Fixed premiums that never increase
  • Tax-deferred growth potential
  • Policy loans available against cash value
Learn More

Indexed Universal Life (IUL)

Flexible permanent coverage with cash value growth linked to market index performance (without direct market risk). Build tax-advantaged wealth while maintaining death benefit protection.

  • Downside protection with upside potential
  • Flexible premium payments
  • Tax-free access to cash value (via loans/withdrawals)
  • Living benefits for critical/chronic illness
  • Estate planning and legacy building tool
Schedule Consultation

Annuities

Safe-money retirement solutions that provide guaranteed income and protect against market losses

Fixed Annuities

Guaranteed interest rates and principal protection. Convert a lump sum into predictable, guaranteed lifetime income streams.

  • Principal protection (no market risk)
  • Guaranteed minimum interest rates
  • Tax-deferred growth
  • Guaranteed lifetime income options
  • No annual fees or management costs
Get Started

Fixed Index Annuities (FIA)

Participate in market-linked gains while protecting your principal from losses. Earn interest based on index performance with a guaranteed floor.

  • Market upside with downside protection
  • Principal guaranteed against market losses
  • Multiple crediting strategies available
  • Optional income riders for guaranteed lifetime income
  • Tax-deferred accumulation
